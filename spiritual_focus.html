Spiritual Focus

1.Spirituality is a broad term that refers to a person's search for meaning, purpose, and connection to something greater than themselves.

2. At its core, spirituality involves exploring questions about the nature of existence, the meaning of life, and the relationship between the individual and the universe.

3 Spiritual means relating to people's thoughts and beliefs, rather than to their bodies and physical surroundings

4 Spirituality awakens within a person when they attain a certain peace of mind. They become one with their body, mind and soul. When nothing scares or attracts them, or when they don't easily get swayed away by human emotions, that's when a person attains spirituality. The spiritual state of mind can be very relaxing.
